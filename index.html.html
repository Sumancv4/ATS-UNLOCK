Frequently asked

What is an ATS and why does it matter? ⌄
An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by 98% of Fortune 500 companies to filter CVs before a human ever sees them. If your CV doesn't match the job description's keywords and formatting, it gets rejected automatically — even if you're perfectly qualified.
How accurate is the ATS score? ⌄
Our scoring algorithm analyses keyword density, semantic matching, formatting compatibility, section structure, and role-specific terminology. It's calibrated against real ATS systems including Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, and Taleo — achieving 91% correlation in A/B testing.
Can I cancel at any time? ⌄
Yes — no contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard at any time and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. We don't make it hard to leave because we're confident you won't want to.
Is my CV data kept private? ⌄
Absolutely. Your CV data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your personal information. You can delete all your data from your account settings at any time. We're GDPR compliant and UK-based.

Yes — we offer Enterprise plans for recruitment agencies, career coaches, and university career services. These include bulk CV analysis, white-labelling, candidate dashboards, and dedicated account management. Contact us for a custom quote.
